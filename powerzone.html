Powerzone is the only metal radio show of its kind in the UK! Playing the most brutal metal on British airwaves, you won't find another show like it.

As a long time friend of the host Dani "The Gentleman of Metal", and occasional guest, I was asked if I would like to help out by interviewing bands when they visit London. How could I say no?! Known as "The Beard" on air, I have started gathering contacts in the music industry and interviewing artists. I'm always happy for feedback so please get in touch and let me know what you think!

Powerzone is aired on CalonFM every Monday from 21:00 UTC. Get in touch with The Gentleman of Metal on twitter or facebook.


You can check out all my interviews on SoundCloud. Thanks to Matt from PodPro for post-production assistance.
