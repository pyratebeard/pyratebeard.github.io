Melksham Comic Con is a not-for-profit organisation, started in 2013 between a group of comic geek friends. Our convention was created to try and bring our passion to others, a convention for the fans, by the fans.

I have helped organise and work the convention every year. After the first year I took on the website responsibilities as my primary duty. Now I am a Director on the Board and the Technical Officer.

For more information check out our website, or email me: tech [at] melkshamcomiccon [dot] com.
